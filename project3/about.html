Janis Rebecca Ahn is a graphic designer and photographer residing in the greater Los Angeles area. She is currently a third year undergraduate student attending Otis College of Art and Design studying Communication Arts with an emphasis in graphic design.

Janis cares about functional, impactful, beautiful design. She believes strongly in the power of communication through visual language and the importance of creating work with the intention of helping the world and others. She loves meeting new people, collaborating, and most importantly, learning.

Outside of her design focused work she enjoys taking photos, video, cooking delicious plant based dishes, playing the bass in her Christian band, and petting every dog in sight.
